      \multicolumn{2}{l}{DDL \citep{zhang2026ddl}}                          & $\mathbf{H}_{l} = (\mathbf{I} {-} \beta_{l} \bm{k}_{l}\bm{k}_{l}^\top)\mathbf{H}_{l-1} + \beta_{l} \bm{k}_{l}\bm{v}_{l}^\top$                                                                & Dynamic                                                                                                                   & $d_v$ streams                                                                    \\
